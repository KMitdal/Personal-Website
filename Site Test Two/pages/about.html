About Myself

My name is Kennay Mitdal. I am a digital artist predominantly focused on digital illustrations as well as in the creative part of the advertising industry. You may have some some of my work prominently featured in art galleries in Arizona, where I chose to reside, or on the television. I work with many different mediums of digital goods from photography, digital painting, sound, video and photo editing. I've built my skills out of my passion for creative development and I have hoped that I would be an inspiration to bring and guide more people into such a rewarding field of work. I even occassionally stream my creative processes with the world to see!. If you're looking to get a piece I've made currently for yourself, please visit my shop. but if custom work is you speed, please visit my contact page and I would love to hear about your ideas!
